All about Lenabtaker

Lenabtaker is an entrepreneurship competition that focuses on innovation and teaches the youth the way of creating a business by developing new ideas that will help people.

What is Timesync?

Timesync was created due to issues with the youth of time management and procrastination so we’ve developed a unique solution that blends productivity, gamification, and collaboration. The app will consist of all these features to help students.

We Qualified!

Our team successfully qualified in the Lenabtaker competition, advancing with our innovative Timesync app that empowers students through smart productivity tools.
